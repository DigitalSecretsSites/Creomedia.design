# CreoMedia Website Overview

## Website Summary
CreoMedia is a digital marketing agency website built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The site features a modern, responsive design focused on showcasing marketing services, portfolio, and client success stories. The site follows a consistent design language across all pages with a focus on conversion and lead generation.

## Page Structure

### 1. Home Page (`index.html`)
**Role:** Main landing page that introduces the agency and its services
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Fixed navigation with logo, menu items, and CTA button
- **Hero Section:** Large banner with headline, description, CTA buttons, and social links
- **Services Section:** Grid of service cards showcasing marketing services (SEO, PPC, Social Media, etc.)
- **Features Section:** Highlighted benefits with icons and descriptions
- **Project/Portfolio Section:** Filterable project showcase with category tags
- **Clients Section:** Client logos and testimonials
- **Newsletter Section:** Subscription form with background image
- **Blog Section:** Latest marketing insights and articles
- **Team Section:** Team member cards with dual images and social links
- **Footer:** Multi-column layout with site links, social media, and Instagram feed

### 2. Services Page (`services.html`)
**Role:** Detailed overview of marketing services offered
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Consistent navigation across all pages
- **Services Hero:** Dedicated hero section for services
- **Process Section:** Step-by-step marketing approach with 4 stages
- **Newsletter Section:** Subscription for marketing insights
- **Clients Section:** Trusted client logos
- **Blog Section:** Marketing insights related to services
- **Footer:** Consistent footer layout

### 3. Features Page (`features.html`)
**Role:** Highlights agency's unique selling points and capabilities
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Standard navigation
- **Features Section:** Core benefits with icons and descriptions
- **Testimonials Section:** Client success stories with ratings
- **Stats Section:** Key performance metrics and achievements
- **FAQ Section:** Common questions with expandable answers
- **CTA Section:** Newsletter subscription with background image
- **Footer:** Standard footer with navigation

### 4. Portfolio Page (`portfolio.html`)
**Role:** Showcases completed projects and case studies
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Consistent branding
- **Portfolio Hero:** Introduction to portfolio section
- **Stats Section:** Key metrics highlighting agency success
- **Project Gallery:** Filterable project showcase with category tags
- **Blog Section:** Related marketing insights
- **Footer:** Standard multi-column footer

### 5. Blog Page (`blog.html`)
**Role:** Content hub for marketing insights and industry trends
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Consistent navigation
- **Blog Hero:** Introduction to marketing insights
- **Newsletter Section:** Subscription for updates
- **Blog List:** Grid of blog articles with featured post at top
- **Footer:** Standard footer with site navigation

### 6. Contact Page (`contact.html`)
**Role:** Facilitates communication between prospects and the agency
**Design Layout:**
- **Header:** Standard navigation
- **Contact Hero:** Introduction and CTA
- **Contact Info Section:** Office details, contact methods, and business hours
- **Contact Form:** Detailed form for inquiries with validation
- **Map Section:** Location visualization (placeholder)
- **Footer:** Consistent footer layout

## Common Design Elements

### Navigation
- Responsive mobile-friendly navigation with hamburger menu
- Consistent across all pages
- Includes CTA button in header
- Active page highlighting

### Footer
- Four-column layout with:
  - Company information and description
  - Quick links to all main pages
  - Additional navigation options
  - Instagram feed with 6 recent posts
- Copyright and legal links
- Social media integration

### Visual Components
- **Cards:** Used throughout for services, projects, blog posts, and team members
- **Grid Layouts:** Responsive grid systems for content organization
- **Filter Systems:** Category filtering for projects and services
- **Testimonials:** Client feedback sections with avatars
- **Stats Displays:** Animated counters for key metrics
- **Image Galleries:** Project showcases and blog featured images

### Interactive Elements
- **Newsletter Forms:** Email subscription across multiple pages
- **FAQ Accordion:** Expandable question sections
- **Hover Effects:** Dynamic hover animations on cards and buttons
- **Social Media Links:** Integrated throughout the site
- **Back-to-Top Button:** Sticky navigation aid

## Target Audience
The website is designed for businesses seeking marketing services, particularly those interested in:
- SEO and search marketing
- Pay-per-click advertising
- Social media marketing
- Content marketing
- Email marketing
- Brand development
- Analytics and reporting

## Technical Features
- Responsive design for all device sizes
- Accessibility features (skip links, semantic HTML, ARIA labels)
- Performance optimization (lazy loading, efficient images)
- SEO best practices (meta tags, structured data, canonical URLs)
- Cross-browser compatibility
- Modern JavaScript functionality (form validation, animations)
